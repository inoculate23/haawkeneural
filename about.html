Haawke: AI for A/V
  • 1991
    Debut of our 3D spatial audio system
    We introduced our spatial audio system in Francisco, California on New Years Eve at The Design Center, at Toontown. We blew some minds.
  • 1992
    Tours with Moby, Psychic TV, and more.
    Released four audio cd's on Silent Records, one of the best labels for ambient music in those days.
  • 1992
    Burning Man
    We founded the music element of Burning Man. We were the first sound camp and DJ's. We still burn and DJ.
  • 1993
    India
    Lived in India for 6 months making music with trancemasters from around the world.
  • 1996
    Interned at DV Garage
    Interned at San Francisco's DV Garage. Under the mentorship of Alex Lindsey (Ex-Rebel Mac Unit at Industrial Light and Magic) I learned commercially viable techniques using 3D and motion graphics software.
  • 2003
    12 years in paradise
    Owned and operated Craig Ellenwood Photography in Honolulu, Hawaii from 2003-2015
  • 2015
    Inoculate Media
    Awarded a provincial grant and launched Inoculate.Media in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.
    Built video processing pipelines, transcoders, and applications for the TV and film industry. Started learning about ai and machine learning. Producer at Vivo Media Arts.
  • 2022
    Las Vegas, Nevada
    Relocated to Las Vegas and created large scale projection mapping and immersive environments at Area 15.
    Continued building knowledge of ai and machine learning and how they apply to video.
  • 2023
    Haawke Neural Technology
    Launched Haawke Neural Technology to bring ai and machine learning applications for events and performance to the world on a grand scale.
