About PRIFF

Celebrating Global Cinema

The Point Roberts International Film Festival (PRIFF) is an annual celebration of cinematic artistry, bringing together filmmakers, industry professionals, and film enthusiasts from around the world.

Our mission is to showcase diverse voices, foster creative collaboration, and provide a platform for emerging talent to shine alongside established filmmakers.
